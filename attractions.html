Happy Clappy

Your "home-away-from-home"

This immaculate four-bedroom upper maisonette is finished to an extremely high standard throughout and benefits from high ceilings, excellent room proportions and an abundance of light throughout.

The second floor presents three double bedrooms, one with en suite bathroom and a family shower room. The master bedroom has a sizeable en suite bathroom as well as a separate dressing room with fitted cupboards, providing plenty of storage space. The dressing room can also be accessed from the second bedroom.

The upper floor comprises a large open plan reception/dining room which is well laid out and encompasses a large modern fitted kitchen area with a breakfast bar and separate utility room and guest cloakroom. There is ample living and entertaining space and a further double bedroom/home office can also be found on this level.
